Welcome to 
Wicklow Christmas Market

Get ready to embrace the festive spirit! It's time to hang up your stockings, adorn the tree with sparkling tinsel, and enjoy some mulled wine  beneath the mistletoe. Christmas is just around the corner, and in Wicklow, that means it's time for the renowned Wicklow Christmas Market. Prepare to be enchanted by the magic that the market has in store for Christmas enthusiasts of all ages. 

At the Wicklow Christmas Market, we believe that the festive cheer should be accessible to everyone. That's why, as always, entry to the market is FREE. Once you step inside, you'll be transported into a breathtaking Christmas wonderland. Explore a world of delectable international cuisine from local food vendors, browse through 40+ craft stalls to check off your Christmas shopping list, and be captivated by the legendary fireshow and live entertainment. Come and see for yourself what all the excitement is about!"

Explore the Market

Discover the ultimate destination for your shopping, dining, and entertainment desires at Our Market. Whether you're hunting for Christmas presents to delight your loved ones or seeking a little something special for yourself, Our Market boasts over 40 craft stalls brimming with one-of-a-kind gems for you to explore this year. And when your shopping spree inevitably builds up your appetite, fear not! We've got a diverse array of food stalls to satiate every craving. Excitingly, we're introducing a new feature this year—a covered entertainment area where you can revel in live music, puppet performances, stilt walkers, and a few delightful surprises!

The Jingle Bell Express

"Step aboard The Jingle Bell Express train for a magical journey to the North Pole and a delightful visit with Santa. Our dedicated elves have been hard at work constructing a new Elves Workshop, and their craftsmanship has been so impressive that Santa himself was inspired to design a new station for the start of your journey to meet Santa and Mrs. Claus.

As you travel to the North Pole, you'll pass through the enchanting 'Snow Tunnel,' creating a sense of anticipation and wonder before you reach your destination. Upon arrival, Mrs. Claus will extend a warm welcome and treat you to a heartwarming storytime and a joyful sing-along. And, of course, you'll have the incredible opportunity to meet the one and only Santa Claus!"

WHAT’S ON
The Market Experience

Stroll through our market and take in the sights, sounds, and smells of all things Christmas. Be sure to come hungry- it will be hard to resist our many food stalls featuring mouth-watering cuisine from all over the globe. The adults get to do some early Christmas shopping as we will have local artisans selling handcrafted ornaments, paintings, toys, homemade chutneys and jams, and so much more that you won’t be able to find at your typical store. The kiddies will be entertained feeding the alpacas, enjoying the carnival rides and playing games for prizes at the Fun Fair, and of course visiting Santa and Mrs. Claus. Create memories to last a lifetime and kick off the Christmas season with a day at the Wicklow Christmas Market!

Wicklow Christmas Market is proud to be a Green Festival using recycled and sustainable materials. Our stallholders follow the “leave no trace” policy, and we keep the environment and its protection on the forefront when planning and constructing the market.

Check us out on Facebook or Instagram closer to the time for more details of the stall holders joining us for each of the dates of the Wicklow Christmas Market.

The Market Experience
  • The Jingle Bell Express

    A day at the Wicklow Christmas Market would not be complete without a magical trip on The Jingle Bell Express, transporting you and your family all the way to the North Pole and the home of Santa and Mrs. Claus. Get ready for a fun ride with Santa’s favourite mischievous (but fun!) elf, Oribel, as your train conductor.

    The experience begins at The Jingle Bell Express train station. From the Ticket Office Oribel, our playful Ticket Conductor will greet guests and introduce them to the Train driver.  Festive music will welcome travellers as The Conductor checks and punches tickets before the train sets of for an adventure to Santa's Cabin.
